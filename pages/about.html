Our story


At Wesley, we began with a love for dogs and a vision to enhance their lives with
stylish, high-quality accessories. What started as a small team of passionate dog owners has
grown into a brand dedicated to combining functionality with fashion. Each product, from
comfortable collars to durable toys, is crafted with care to bring joy and convenience to dogs
and their owners alike.

Founded in 2023, we remain committed to innovation and quality, ensuring every dog gets the
best. Join us in celebrating the special bond between you and your furry friend with accessories
designed to make every moment memorable.
