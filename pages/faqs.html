FAQs

What size dog collar should I choose for my pet?

To find the perfect fit for your dog’s collar, measure the circumference of their neck and add
1-2 inches for comfort. Consult our sizing guide available on each product page for detailed
measurements and recommendations. If your dog is between sizes, we suggest opting for the larger
size for better adjustability.

How do I properly fit a harness on my dog?

To ensure a proper fit, adjust the straps of the harness so that you can fit two fingers between
the harness and your dog’s body. The harness should be snug but not too tight. Make sure it’s
evenly distributed across your dog’s chest and shoulders to prevent chafing. For detailed
fitting instructions, refer to our harness sizing guide.

Can I use a leash with both a collar and a harness?

Yes, you can use a leash with both collars and harnesses. For everyday walks, a harness may
be more comfortable for your dog and provide better control. However, if your dog is trained to
walk with a collar, using a leash with the collar is perfectly fine. Ensure that the leash is
securely attached and appropriate for your dog’s size and strength.

How can I purchase a gift card and use it for online shopping?

You can purchase a gift card directly from our website by selecting the "Gift Card" option in our
shop section. Choose the amount you’d like to load onto the card and complete the purchase. Once
you receive the gift card via email, you can enter the card code during checkout to redeem its
value. Gift cards can be used for any product in our online store.
